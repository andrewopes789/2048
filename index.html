About
Restate is a real estate analytics visualization application designed for use by first-time homebuyers and experienced property investors alike.
By simply entering a zip code, receive real-time analytics about the area's properties' average sale price, median sale price, and number of sales, segmented by month. Access historical trends by requesting data from a previous year.
